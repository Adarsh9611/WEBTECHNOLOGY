Breaking News:SMVITM Autonomus

See all links Images Videos Shri Madhwa Vadiraja Institute of Technology and Management (SMVITM), Bantakal, has been granted autonomous status by the UGC for 10 years starting from the academic year 2025–26, allowing the institute to independently design and manage its curricula and academic processes. Daijiworld +1 Key Highlights of Autonomous Status Curriculum Flexibility: With autonomy, SMVITM can design its own undergraduate (UG) and postgraduate (PG) programs in alignment with the NEP 2020 guidelines, providing flexibility to introduce innovative courses, update syllabi regularly, and adopt modern teaching methodologies Daijiworld . Academic Oversight: The institute will oversee all academic processes, including teaching–learning methods, assessment and evaluation of student performance, and curriculum decisions. Degrees will continue to be awarded by the affiliating university, Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU), based on the institute’s recommendations Daijiworld . Accreditations and Rankings: All eligible UG BE programs—Computer Science & Engineering, Electronics & Communication Engineering, and Mechanical Engineering—have received NBA accreditation. The institute is also accredited by NAAC with an A Grade and has been recognized in the NIRF Innovation Ranking within the 151–300 band Daijiworld . Infrastructure and Innovation: SMVITM has strengthened its teaching–learning ecosystem with modern infrastructure. Additionally, the institute has established the “Madhwa Incubator for Technology, Innovation and Entrepreneurship (Mi-TiE)” to support student start-ups, technology-driven projects, and entrepreneurial ventures. It has also been recognized by the Ministry of MSME as a host Business Incubator Daijiworld . Recognition and Milestones: Achieving autonomous status marks SMVITM as the youngest institution in the coastal region of Karnataka to receive such recognition within 15 years of establishment, highlighting its commitment to quality and innovation in education Daijiworld +1 . This autonomous status empowers SMVITM to introduce programs and initiatives tailored to emerging technologies and industry needs, nurturing innovation, entrepreneurship, and holistic development among students Daijiworld . For more details, you can visit the official website of SMVITM here or view media reports on autonomy recognition Daijiworld +1 .
